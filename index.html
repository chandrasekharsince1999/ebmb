Abstract:

The use of internet in day to day life is increasing exponentially in today's world. Pharmaceutical industries are marketing their drugs and other medicines extensively on social networking sites and other platforms. With the easy availability of information, there also exists the vulnerability to misinformation in online pharmaceutical marketing. Five converging factors has been identified by the researchers, they are: 1. Internet dependence, 2. excessive trust in the veracity of online information, 3. unawareness of pharmaceutical company influence, 4. social isolation and 5. detail fixation. As the Internet continues to change, it is important that regulators keep in mind not only misinformation that surrounds new web technologies and their contents. The market of pharmaceutical industries is big online thus, there is a huge probability of the threat to misinformation in online marketing. As this industry is crucial and has a direct impact on public health, it is important to restrict such industries from doing such practices. In this paper, we have reviewed verifi2 software, which is helpful in detecting misinformation on different online platforms.
